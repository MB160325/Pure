Privacy Policy

How Pure Talent Recruitment & Manpower Supply collects, uses, protects, and manages personal information submitted through our website, email, recruitment forms, and business enquiries.

Privacy Policy / سياسة الخصوصية

Effective Date: 13 June 2026

Pure Talent Recruitment & Manpower Supply respects the privacy of candidates, clients, partners, and website visitors. This policy explains the type of information we may collect and how it is handled in a professional and responsible manner.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information such as full name, nationality, phone number, email address, position applied for, CV details, work experience, professional qualifications, messages, and business enquiry details.

2. How We Use Information

  • To review job applications and recruitment enquiries.
  • To communicate with candidates and clients.
  • To provide recruitment, staffing, manpower supply, and HR support services.
  • To improve our website, service quality, and client response process.
  • To comply with applicable legal, regulatory, and administrative requirements.

3. CVs and Candidate Documents

Applicants may send CVs or supporting documents by email. These documents are used only for recruitment-related review, communication, shortlisting, and placement activities.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Candidate details may be shared with relevant clients or authorized partners only when required for recruitment, manpower supply, assessment, onboarding, or compliance purposes.

5. Data Security

We apply reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect personal data from unauthorized access, misuse, loss, or disclosure. No online transmission can be guaranteed as completely secure, but we take privacy seriously.

6. Cookies and Website Analytics

The website may use standard browser technologies or analytics tools to improve performance and user experience. Visitors may manage cookie preferences through their browser settings.

7. Data Retention

We retain information for as long as necessary for recruitment, business, compliance, or legitimate administrative purposes, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

8. Your Rights

You may contact us to request access, correction, or deletion of your personal information, subject to applicable legal and operational requirements.

9. Contact

For privacy-related requests, contact us at info@puretalent.ae or call 02 666 0935.
